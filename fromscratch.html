How to Get Scuba Certified

To get scuba certified, start by signing up for beginner certification course through an organization like PADI or SSI. I am SSI certified. This course includes the basic scuba safety and equipment. It also involves practicing skills in a pool then completing a few open water dives with an instructor. During this process, you learn how to breathe underwater safely, clear your mask, control your buoyancy, and respond to common situations. Once you finish the classwork, pool training, and required dives, you earn your certification and can dive with one or more other certified divers in approved conditions.
